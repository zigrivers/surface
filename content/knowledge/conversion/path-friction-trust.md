## Summary

Conversion paths should make the next action, cost, risk, and recovery path clear before users
commit effort or sensitive information.

## Deep Guidance

Inspect critical paths for friction that blocks forward motion: hidden fees, late surprises,
ambiguous calls to action, missing trust cues near sensitive fields, premature validation, and
secondary actions that distract from the primary task. Findings should tie friction to a specific
step in the path and recommend a bounded fix such as exposing total cost earlier, clarifying the
primary action, moving reassurance near a risky field, or making validation feedback immediate
and recoverable.
