## Summary

Task-critical web copy should be clear, concise, organized, and appropriate for the audience.

## Deep Guidance

Use short sentences, meaningful headings, familiar words, and logical organization for labels,
instructions, and other microcopy. Treat long, jargon-heavy sentences as usability risks when
they block users from understanding the next action.
